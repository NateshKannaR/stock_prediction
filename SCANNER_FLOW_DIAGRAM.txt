╔══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╗
║                            KEY DECISION POINTS                               ║
╚══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╝

1. STOCK SELECTION
   ├─ Scanner finds 5 candidates
   ├─ Filter: Only BUY/SELL signals (not HOLD)
   ├─ Filter: Price ≤ 50% of capital
   └─ Pick: Highest intraday_score

2. POSITION SIZING
   ├─ Max per trade: 20% of capital
   ├─ Min quantity: 1 share
   └─ Cost = quantity × price

3. EXIT TRIGGERS
   ├─ Profit target: +1.0% (configurable)
   ├─ Stop-loss: -0.5% (configurable)
   ├─ Time-based: 3:15 PM (auto square-off)
   └─ Daily loss limit: ₹2,000 (halts trading)

4. RISK CONTROLS
   ├─ Max 1 open position at a time
   ├─ Paper trading mode available
   ├─ Daily loss limit enforcement
   └─ Market hours check (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM)
